❓ Sawal: Agar Allah ka zikr karne ka dil na kare to kya karein?
✅ Jawab: Allah se dua karo, chhoti chhoti tasbeeh se shuru karo, Qur’an ki tilawat karo, nek logon ki sohbat ikhtiyar karo, aur sabr ke sath zikr karte raho. Allah farmata hai: “Alaa bi zikrillahi tatma’innul quloob” (Surah Ra’d: 28)
